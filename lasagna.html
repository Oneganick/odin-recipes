Best Lasagna

Lasagna

"Noodles with lots of beef and tomatos"

Ingredients

Noodles

Beef

Tomato Sauce

Steps

Step 1: Cook the beef with Italian seasoning

Step 2: Boil the noodles in hot salted water

Step 3: Add the cooked noodles, tomato sauce, and cooked beef to a baking sheet

Step 4: Bake at 400 Degrees for 400 Minutes
